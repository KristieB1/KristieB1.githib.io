Kristie Brandis

I am a Senior Quality Analyst, in my current role I manage test strategy and planning. Quality assurance is an integral part of the software development lifecycle. It is the process of verifying software products against business requirements and identifying potential issues before release. My passion is in implementing best practise quality assurance to ensure we deliver effective and reliable systems to team members and customers. I applied to Shecodes to gain knowledge on coding language and practise which I can use to enhance QA process. I would like to thank the team at Shecodes for this opportunity. This site will give you an overview of my Shecodes portfolio, i hope you enjoy.

I live in West Australia, with my husband and our extended family of pets (some are pictured below). In my spare time i am learning the play the piano and i love to spend time reading and completing puzzles. In fact i have a room dedicated to storing my large collection of sci-fi and fantasy novels. My favourite authors include: N.K.Jemison, Brandon Sanderson and Ian Irvine.
